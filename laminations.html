How to Use Lamination Creator

Getting Started

  • Provide API Key: The first time you load the app, you'll be prompted for a Google AI Gemini API key. This is required to generate background images.
  • Enter Text: Type the text you want to use for your lamination into the text area at the bottom. This will be the main text overlaid on your image.
  • Create Lamination: Click the "Create Lamination" button. The app will generate a unique background image and place your text on it.

Customizing Your Lamination

  • Change Background: Below the generated lamination, you'll find an input field labeled "Background". Enter a new description (e.g., "a snowy mountain landscape" or "a vibrant abstract pattern") and click "Generate" to create a new background image while keeping your text.
  • Text Styling: Use the dropdowns and buttons to change the font family, font size, and text color. You can also apply bold, italic, and underline formatting.
  • Positioning Text: The text box on the lamination is interactive! You can:
    • Drag: Click and drag the text box to move it anywhere on the image.
    • Resize: Drag the edges or corners of the text box to change its size.
    • Rotate: On touch devices, use a two-finger rotation gesture. With a mouse, drag outside the corners while holding shift to rotate.

Saving Your Lamination

  • Save Lamination: Click this button to open a dialog with two saving options:
    • 4-Up Print Sheet: Saves a single PNG image containing four copies of your lamination, formatted for easy printing on a standard sheet.
    • Full Size PNG: Saves your lamination as a single, high-resolution PNG image, perfect for sharing online or using as is.

Start Over

  • New Lamination: Clears the current lamination and allows you to start fresh.
